# Learning — TVF Creative Audit & Next Steps

**Source:** TVF (creative agency) · **Date:** 25 June 2026 · **Status:** 🟢 open / to apply
**Objective:** Capture immediate content-level improvements to apply across current assets, and align next steps with TVF.

## Key audit takeaway
The **statics that are working** are largely coming from **direct consumer insights**. The same
sharpness is **not** consistently coming through in **videos**. Current videos need stronger
**character, context, relatability, and story within the first 10–15 seconds.**

## Immediate content-level changes to apply (share with agency now)

**A. Make the insight sharper.** Every creative starts from one clear consumer insight/thought.
*e.g. "Normal net charges for the full month even when need is only for a few days."*

**B. Make the character sharper.** The person shown must match the problem.
- Young man’s problem → young man visible · Delivery boy → bike/context visible · Auto driver → auto visible · Thela wala → thela/context visible.
- The viewer should immediately get: who is this person, and why is this problem relevant to them.

**C. Make the setting sharper.** Avoid random faces in isolation. Show the person in their real
environment so the ad feels believable — *why* he’s relevant, *where* he is, *what* situation he’s in.

**D. Fix vox-pop / testimonial authenticity.** Guardrails:
- Avoid dubbed-looking voices · avoid random close-up headshots · avoid AI vox-pop/testimonials unless clearly spoof/satire · camera not too close without context.
- **Build the character before the line is delivered.** If walking: show them walking → stopping → reacting → *then* speaking — not a direct face-to-camera line.

**E. Add more story inside 10–15 sec.** A video captures a small *situation*, not just a line. Quickly establish: **who is the person? · what is the need/problem? · what is the Wiom angle? · what is the hook/punchline?**

**F. Try the "normal net vs ghar ka net" comparison format.** Make the category difference sharper —
Wiom isn’t just another normal internet option, it’s a different way of getting *ghar ka net*. Test and read.

## What this is, in framework terms
Most of these sharpen the **existing framework** on one of: **consumer insight · right character
for the insight · right setting/context · story moment · the Wiom/category point landed · hook.**
